Privacy Policy for Word Hunters Wear

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MarCas Code ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the smartwatch application Word Hunters Wear.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Information

When using the app, you may choose to provide a username. This username is used for gameplay identification and leaderboard display.

b. Usage and Device Data

We use Firebase services to collect information such as:

  • Gameplay activity (levels completed, scores, time spent)
  • Leaderboard participation data (scores and rankings)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

c. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store payment details, but we may receive confirmation of purchases (such as premium unlock status).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain game features
  • Display usernames and scores on leaderboards
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Manage in-app purchases

3. Public Information

Your username and game scores may be visible to other players through in-app leaderboards. Please avoid using personal or sensitive information as your username.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services including Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google Play Services. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain gameplay data, usernames, and leaderboard information for as long as necessary to provide the app’s functionality. Analytics and crash data may be retained for a limited time by Firebase.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures and rely on trusted platforms such as Firebase and Google Play to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data, including your username and associated game data, by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at marcascode@gmail.com.
